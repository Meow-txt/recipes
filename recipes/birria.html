Beef Birria

Description:

This is a Mexican stew made with blended chile peppers simmered with beef stew meat.

Ingredients:

  • 5 dried Anaheim chile peppers, stemmed and seeded
  • 5 guajillo chile peppers, stemmed and seeded.
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 tablespoon mixed spices
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 3 lbs. cubed beef stew meat
  • 6 bay leaves

Steps:

  1. Place Anaheim and guajillo peppers in a saucepan; cover with water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes. Set aside to cool for 5 minutes.
  2. Transfer chiles and water into a blender; add onion, mixed spices, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  3. Place stew meat in a large pot; stir in pureed chile mixture and add bay leaves. Cook over medium-low heat until meat is very tender, 3-5 hours.
