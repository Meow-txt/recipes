Lasagna

Description:

Cheesy, tomatoe-y, layered pasta goodness.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: parsley, basil, salt, Italian Seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese.
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasgna rest before serving.
